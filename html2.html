                           #coastalareasurvivals

As a group of passionate and driven students, our collective mission revolves around the urgent need to raise awareness and advocate for the preservation of lesser-known coastal sites that brim with profound historical significance. While these coastal locations might not boast widespread recognition, they harbor stories and legacies that weave an intricate tapestry of our past. These places, often hidden in the folds of history, hold the keys to a wealth of narratives, cultural heritage, and bygone tales that are waiting to be discovered and cherished.


Our aim is to shine a light on these hidden gems, where the whispers of time echo through the winds, and where the footprints of ancient civilizations, unique ecosystems, and once-thriving communities remain imprinted in the sands. These places are more than just geographical locations; they serve as living testaments to the people, cultures, and events that have shaped our collective identity.
